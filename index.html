About Jan Kalyan Maha Samiti

Established on 26th January 1988, Jan Kalyan Maha Samiti is a registered society dedicated to grassroots development and social welfare in Uttar Pradesh. For nearly four decades, we have worked tirelessly in the fields of women's health and empowerment, child care and protection, and land development and rights awareness.

Operating primarily from Fatehpur, JKMS has successfully implemented numerous community-centric programs. We have partnered with various government departments and reputed organizations to strengthen public health systems, especially in the area of HIV/AIDS awareness and prevention. Our organization has been actively involved in Targeted Intervention (TI) programs in Fatehpur and Varanasi, worked with Apollo Foundation for trucker health programs, collaborated with Bhumi Sudhar Nigam for land-related initiatives, and extended Childline services across the state.

Our core philosophy remains simple yet powerful — "Empowering communities to become self-reliant." Through sustained efforts in maternal and child health, women's skill development, livelihood support, and land resource management, we continue to bridge the gap between government schemes and the last mile beneficiaries.
