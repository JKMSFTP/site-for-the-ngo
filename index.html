About JKMS

A grassroots movement born on Republic Day, 1988

Jan Kalyan Maha Samiti (JKMS) stands as a beacon of hope for the underprivileged of Uttar Pradesh, particularly in and around Fatehpur. Founded by socially conscious individuals, JKMS has grown into a respected registered society committed to holistic community welfare.

Our work spans women's health, child protection, land rights awareness, and HIV/AIDS prevention — always at the grassroots, always in collaboration with communities themselves.

Women's Health & Economic Empowerment
Child Care, Protection & Education
Land Rights & Sustainable Livelihoods
HIV/AIDS Prevention (Fatehpur & Varanasi)
Disaster Response & Community Rehabilitation

Our vision: every individual, irrespective of caste or economic background, has equal access to opportunity, dignity, and development.

Director's Message

"
For over 37 years, Jan Kalyan Maha Samiti has stood as a beacon of hope for the underprivileged of Fatehpur and surrounding regions. What began as a modest initiative on Republic Day in 1988 has now blossomed into a trusted organization that has touched thousands of lives.

Our commitment to women's health, child welfare, land rights awareness, and overall community development has remained strong and consistent. I have personally witnessed the power of collective effort and compassionate action in transforming lives. Real change always begins at the grassroots level — in the villages, in the homes, and in the hearts of the people we serve.

As we look to the future, let us renew our pledge to work with even greater dedication. Together, we shall continue building a more equitable, healthy, and empowered Uttar Pradesh. Your continued trust and participation are the greatest strength of Jan Kalyan Maha Samiti.
B.P. Pandey
Director & Chief Functionary, JKMS Fatehpur
