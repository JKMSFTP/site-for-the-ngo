About Jan Kalyan Maha Samiti

Established on January 26, 1988, and functioning continuously as a highly accountable registered Society, Jan Kalyan Maha Samiti (JKMS) has dedicated close to four decades toward absolute grassroots transformation across Uttar Pradesh. Operating out of Fatehpur, the society has built a robust framework centered strictly on long-term women's health, comprehensive childcare systems, and structured land development projects. Driven by the mission to convert social policy into sustainable reality, JKMS has executed wide-scale interventions including targeted health access infrastructure, critical maternal safety clinics, and agricultural land management programs designed to empower marginal farmers and rural housewives. Over the years, the organization has consistently stepped up to execute high-stakes welfare initiatives—ranging from intensive field-level health drives to specialized community security measures—consistently expanding its operational footprint across districts to reach underprivileged, isolated clusters with complete institutional transparency and unyielding social commitment.
